64. The Green-Eyed Prisoners

One hundred green-eyed prisoners have been trapped on an island by a mad dictator.
The only way to escape is if they approach the guards at night and ask to leave, assuming they have green eyes.
Each prisoner can see the others' eyes but does not know their own.
You may say **one sentence** that does not introduce new information.
If a prisoner approaches the guards but lack green eyes, they are thrown into a volcano.
What do you say to ensure all 100 prisoners escape safely?
