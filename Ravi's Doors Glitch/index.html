Glitch (GL)
Doors 58-76

Raviolo and his team find themselves in a devious trap. As they fall into a dark chamber, they land next to a sinister-looking bomb. But this time, it's not Door's doing. Someone else is pulling the strings, sabotaging the heroes and Door's plans alike.

To stop the bomb, they must solve 19 interconnected modules labeled 58 - 76 (the next chapter of doors had the team not have fallen for this trap). If they fail, the bomb will release a viscous liquid, trapping everyone forever.
